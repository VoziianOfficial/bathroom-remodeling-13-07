        01

        Overview

        Cookies and similar technologies can support website functionality, remember preferences, help maintain security, and provide information about website operation.

        This policy applies to browser technologies used on the BathNice website. It should be read together with the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service .

        In this policy, “BathNice,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to BathNice Matching Group LLC .

        Current implementation

        The website uses essential browser storage to remember whether the cookie-consent banner was accepted or declined. Optional analytics or advertising technologies should not be activated unless they are separately implemented and handled through the appropriate consent process.

        02

        Technology Definitions

        Cookies

        A cookie is a small text record that a website or related service may place in a browser. Cookies can remember a preference, identify a browser session, support security, or assist with website measurement.

        Local storage

        Local storage is a browser feature that allows a website to store a limited amount of information on the device. Unlike a traditional cookie, local-storage information is not automatically included with every web request.

        Session storage

        Session storage is similar to local storage but is generally limited to the active browser tab or session. It may be removed when the tab or browser session ends.

        Server logs

        Hosting and security systems may automatically create technical records when a browser requests a page. These records may include an IP address, request time, browser information, requested page, response status, and error or security information.

        Pixels, tags, and similar scripts

        Some websites use small scripts, tracking pixels, tags, software-development tools, or similar technologies to measure activity or support third-party services. BathNice will update this policy if such optional technologies are materially added to the website.

        03

        How the Current Website Uses Browser Storage

        BathNice’s cookie-consent interface allows a visitor to accept or decline the website’s stated browser-storage choice.

        After a choice is selected, the website may store a small essential preference record in the browser. This allows the website to remember the selection and avoid displaying the banner on every page or every visit.

        The stored preference may include:

        • Whether the consent message was accepted or declined.
        • The version of the consent preference or website implementation.
        • A timestamp associated with the selection.

        The preference record is not intended to contain your name, email address, telephone number, bathroom-project description, or other information submitted through the contact form.

        Consent choice persistence

        The essential preference remains in the browser until it expires, is replaced, is removed by the website, or is deleted through browser settings.

        04

        Technology Categories

        Website technologies are commonly grouped by purpose. The table below explains how these categories relate to BathNice.

        Browser technology categories
        Category Purpose Current status Typical duration
        Essential Remember privacy choices, support core functionality, prevent repeated consent prompts, and maintain basic security. Used where required for the consent preference and website operation. Session-based or persistent until expiration, replacement, or deletion.
        Functional Remember optional interface or personalization preferences beyond strictly necessary behavior. Not intentionally required by the current core implementation. Depends on the feature if later implemented.
        Analytics Measure visits, page usage, traffic sources, errors, and website performance. No optional analytics tool is intentionally activated by the core website files unless one is separately added. Depends on the analytics provider and configuration.

        Security and logs Detect errors, abuse, automated submissions, attacks, or infrastructure problems. Hosting or security systems may create technical logs independently of browser consent storage. Determined by hosting, security, and operational retention settings.

        Technology settings may differ after deployment if a hosting provider, analytics system, advertising tool, or other third-party integration is added.

        05

        Consent Banner and Preference Persistence

        When no saved privacy preference is available, the website may display a cookie-consent banner containing Accept and Decline actions.

        Accept

        Selecting Accept records the accepted choice in browser storage. This prevents the message from repeatedly appearing and can allow optional technologies to be enabled if they are implemented and covered by the consent interface.

        Decline

        Selecting Decline records the declined choice in browser storage. Essential website functions may continue to operate because strictly necessary technologies do not depend on optional consent.

        No selection

        Closing the browser or leaving the website without selecting a choice may cause the banner to appear again during a later visit.

        Resetting a choice

        You can remove the saved preference through browser settings. After the record is deleted, the website may display the consent banner again.

        Changes to consent handling

        BathNice may reset an existing preference when the consent implementation, policy version, or optional technology categories materially change.

        Third-Party Technologies

        The website may load resources from third-party services, such as:

        • Font-delivery networks.
        • JavaScript libraries or content-delivery networks.
        • Mapping or external navigation services.
        • Hosting, email-delivery, spam-prevention, or security providers.
        • Analytics or advertising tools if separately added.

        A third-party service may receive technical information when the browser requests its resource. This can include an IP address, browser information, requested resource, referrer information, and request time.

        BathNice does not control every third party’s browser technology, retention period, privacy policy, or technical system.

        External links, including map links and provider websites, are governed by the policies of their respective operators after you leave BathNice.

        Review external policies

        Before using a third-party website or service, review its privacy, cookie, and terms information.

        07

        Browser and Device Controls

        Most browsers provide controls that allow you to view, block, limit, or delete cookies and site data.

        Depending on the browser, these options may appear under settings labeled:

        • Privacy and security.
        • Cookies and site data.
        • Tracking protection.
        • Storage or website data.
        • Permissions.
        • Clear browsing data.

        Deleting BathNice storage

        You can usually remove stored BathNice data by locating the website in your browser’s site-data settings and deleting its cookies or local-storage records.

        Private browsing

        Private or incognito browsing modes may remove site data when the private session closes, although behavior depends on the browser.

        Device synchronization

        Some browsers synchronize settings across signed-in devices. BathNice does not control whether a browser synchronizes cookie or storage preferences.

        Browser updates

        Browser menus and terminology may change over time. Consult the browser publisher’s current support materials for device-specific instructions.

        08

        Effect of Blocking or Deleting Storage

        Blocking or deleting browser storage may affect how the website remembers your choices.

        Possible effects include:

        • The cookie-consent banner may appear again.
        • A previously selected Accept or Decline choice may be forgotten.
        • Certain optional features may not function if they depend on storage.
        • Security, spam-prevention, or session-based functions may behave differently.

        Removing the consent preference does not automatically delete information previously submitted through the project-request form.

        Requests concerning submitted personal information should be directed through the contact details in the Privacy Policy .

        09

        Retention and Security

        Browser-storage duration

        An essential preference may remain until its configured expiration, until a newer preference replaces it, until BathNice resets the preference version, or until it is deleted through browser controls.

        Server-log duration

        Hosting and security logs may be retained for periods determined by operational, technical, fraud-prevention, troubleshooting, or legal needs.

        Security

        BathNice uses reasonable measures intended to protect website systems and stored preferences. No browser, server, communication channel, or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

        Preference limitations

        Browser-storage records are associated with the browser or device where the selection was made. A choice made on one device may not apply to another browser, profile, or device.
