        01

        Overview

        This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through the BathNice website, contact channels, and bathroom-project request form. It also explains certain choices you may have regarding that information.

        In this policy, “BathNice,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to BathNice Matching Group LLC . “You” refers to a website visitor, homeowner, property representative, or other person who interacts with the platform.

        Read this policy before submitting information

        By using the website or submitting a project request, you acknowledge that your information may be handled as described in this Privacy Policy.

        02

        BathNice’s Platform Role

        BathNice provides an independent platform designed to help homeowners explore bathroom-remodeling categories and request possible introductions to participating independent service providers.

        BathNice is not the contractor, installer, designer, architect, engineer, plumber, electrician, tile installer, accessibility specialist, product manufacturer, or material supplier for a project.

        When you submit a project request, BathNice may review the information and may share relevant portions with one or more participating providers to explore whether a provider may be available and interested in contacting you.

        BathNice may

        • Receive and review project-request information.
        • Contact you for clarification.
        • Explore possible provider connections.
        • Share relevant request details with participating providers.

        BathNice does not

        • Inspect or perform bathroom remodeling work.
        • Guarantee provider availability or contact.
        • Set provider estimates, prices, or contract terms.
        • Become a party to a homeowner-provider agreement.
        03

        Information We May Collect

        3.1 Information you provide directly

        We may collect information that you voluntarily submit through the website, by phone, by email, or through another communication channel.

        • Name and contact details, including email address and telephone number.
        • Project category, such as shower remodeling, bathtub replacement, tile installation, vanity changes, full remodeling, or accessibility-related work.
        • Project description, bathroom condition, requested changes, location context, timing, and other details you choose to provide.
        • Communications, questions, feedback, and support requests.
        • Consent records associated with the request form.

        3.2 Information collected automatically

        When you visit the website, certain technical information may be transmitted automatically by your browser, hosting provider, security tools, or website infrastructure.

        • Internet Protocol address.
        • Browser type, device type, operating system, and language settings.
        • Referring page, requested page, date, time, and basic interaction information.
        • Server logs, error information, and security-related events.
        • Cookie or local-storage preferences necessary to remember consent choices.

        3.3 Sensitive information

        Please do not submit Social Security numbers, government identification numbers, payment-card information, financial-account credentials, medical records, or other highly sensitive information through the project request form.

        Accessibility-related project descriptions may reveal information about how a bathroom is used. Provide only the information reasonably necessary to describe the requested project. BathNice does not request medical diagnoses through the form.

        04

        How We May Use Information

        BathNice may use collected information for purposes including:

        • Receiving, reviewing, and responding to your request.
        • Understanding the project category and requested bathroom work.
        • Determining whether one or more participating providers may be relevant to the request.
        • Sharing relevant request details with participating providers for possible follow-up.
        • Communicating with you by phone, email, or another method you provide.
        • Operating, maintaining, securing, and improving the website.
        • Detecting spam, fraud, abuse, duplicate submissions, technical errors, and security incidents.
        • Maintaining records of communications, consent, and platform activity.
        • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing applicable policies.

        Project-request communications

        When you submit a request and provide contact information, BathNice or a participating provider may use that information to discuss the request. Submitting a request does not guarantee that a provider will contact you.

        05

        How Information May Be Shared

        BathNice may disclose information in the circumstances described below.

        5.1 Participating independent providers

        We may share relevant project-request information with one or more participating providers so they can evaluate potential interest, availability, service-area fit, or whether they wish to contact you.

        Providers are independent businesses. Their handling of information may be governed by their own privacy practices, systems, policies, and legal obligations.

        5.2 Technology and operational vendors

        We may use hosting, email, security, analytics, infrastructure, form-delivery, and technical-support vendors that process information on our behalf or as necessary to provide their services.

        5.3 Legal and safety disclosures

        We may disclose information when we reasonably believe doing so is necessary to:

        • Comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
        • Protect the rights, safety, property, or security of BathNice, website users, providers, or others.
        • Detect, investigate, or prevent fraud, misuse, security incidents, or unlawful activity.
        • Enforce our Terms of Service or other policies.

        5.4 Business transactions

        Information may be transferred or disclosed in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, sale of assets, bankruptcy, or similar business transaction, subject to applicable legal requirements.

        5.5 With your direction or consent

        We may disclose information when you direct us to do so, request a connection, or otherwise provide consent.

        Sale of personal information

        BathNice does not sell personal information in exchange for money. The disclosure of project information to participating providers is used to support the provider-matching service you request.

        06

        Cookies, Local Storage, and Similar Technologies

        The website may use cookies, browser storage, server logs, and similar technologies to support essential functions, remember privacy choices, maintain security, and understand basic website operation.

        Essential storage

        Essential storage may be used to remember whether you accepted or declined the cookie-consent message and to support necessary website behavior.

        Analytics and optional technologies

        If optional analytics or similar technologies are added, their use should be described through the website’s consent interface or Cookie Policy where required.

        Browser controls

        Most browsers allow you to remove or block cookies and local storage. Disabling essential browser storage may cause privacy preferences or certain website functions not to persist.

        For more information, review the Cookie Policy .

        07

        Data Retention and Security

        Retention

        BathNice may retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to process requests, communicate with users, support provider matching, maintain records, prevent misuse, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

        Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the information, the reason it was collected, legal requirements, operational needs, and whether an active request or dispute exists.

        Security

        BathNice uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

        No website, server, transmission method, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Therefore, BathNice cannot guarantee absolute security.

        Email and telephone communications

        Standard email and telephone communications may not be encrypted. Do not send highly sensitive information through ordinary email, voicemail, or the project form.

        08

        Your Choices and Privacy Rights

        Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights concerning personal information, including the ability to request:

        • Access to certain personal information.
        • Correction of inaccurate information.
        • Deletion of certain information.
        • Information about categories of information collected, used, or disclosed.
        • A portable copy of certain information.
        • Restriction of or objection to certain processing.
        • Withdrawal of consent where processing depends on consent.

        These rights are not absolute and may be subject to verification, exceptions, retention requirements, or other limitations under applicable law.

        Submitting a privacy request

        To submit a privacy request, contact BathNice using the information in the Contact Us section. Include enough information for us to understand the request and reasonably verify your identity.

        Authorized agents

        Where permitted by law, an authorized agent may submit a request on your behalf. BathNice may require proof of authorization and direct verification with the person whose information is involved.

        Non-discrimination

        BathNice will not unlawfully discriminate against a person for exercising an applicable privacy right.

        Do Not Track

        Some browsers transmit “Do Not Track” signals. Because no universally accepted standard currently governs how websites should respond to every such signal, the website may not respond to all browser-based Do Not Track settings.

        09

        Children’s Privacy

        BathNice is intended for adults seeking information or possible provider connections for residential bathroom projects. The website is not directed to children under 13 years of age.

        BathNice does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If we learn that such information was submitted, we may take reasonable steps to delete it.

        A parent or legal guardian who believes a child has submitted personal information may contact BathNice using the information below.

        10

        Third-Party Websites and Independent Providers

        The website may contain links to third-party websites, mapping services, provider websites, or other external resources. BathNice does not control those external websites or their privacy practices.

        Participating providers are independent businesses. Information you provide directly to a provider after an introduction may be collected, used, retained, or disclosed under that provider’s own privacy policy and business practices.

        Review the privacy policy and terms of any third-party website or provider before submitting additional information.

        Provider independence

        BathNice does not control an independent provider’s systems, communication methods, record retention, privacy practices, estimates, contracts, or project performance.

        When the policy changes, the revised version will be posted on this page and the “Last updated” date may be changed.

        Your continued use of the website after a revised policy is posted is subject to the updated policy, to the extent permitted by applicable law.
