        Vanity project overview

        A vanity should connect storage, plumbing, countertop, lighting, and daily circulation.

        The request may involve replacing a similar vanity, changing width, adding a second basin, using a floating design, increasing storage, or updating the full wall composition.

        Participating providers may evaluate room dimensions, plumbing positions, wall support, floor and wall conditions, door movement, storage requirements, countertop fabrication, basin compatibility, mirror size, electrical work, and adjacent finishes.

        A useful vanity request considers what must fit inside, above, behind, and around the cabinetry.

        Final dimensions, wall support, plumbing compatibility, countertop fabrication, electrical work, pricing, lead times, and installation responsibilities must be confirmed directly with participating providers.

        Vanity proposal checklist

        Request written clarity on dimensions, products, fabrication, and installation.

        A detailed proposal can help clarify which provider, fabricator, supplier, electrician, plumber, or homeowner is responsible for each part.

        Homeowners should independently verify licensing, insurance, references, applicable requirements, and other provider information before entering into an agreement.

        Confirm vanity width, depth, height, finish, hardware, fillers, panels, mounting method, basin configuration, countertop dimensions, faucet clearances, mirror size, and approved substitutions.

        Ask which existing elements will be removed, how the wall and floor will be protected, whether support or blocking is needed, and which wall, tile, paint, or floor repairs are included.

        Review drain and supply changes, shutoffs, basin installation, faucet mounting, traps, testing, access, drawer conflicts, specialist work, and compatibility responsibilities.

        Clarify who templates, fabricates, transports, installs, supports, seals, and warranties the countertop, cutouts, seams, edges, backsplash, side splashes, and related wall conditions.

        Ask who supplies and installs mirrors, recessed or surface medicine cabinets, sconces, integrated lighting, switches, outlets, electrical boxes, wall repair, and final finish work.

        Review product ordering, delivery, inspection, storage, returns, lead times, allowances, total pricing, payment stages, schedule assumptions, changes, product warranties, and workmanship warranties.
